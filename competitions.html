Competitions We Participate In:

Programming ▼
  • Singapore NOI (National Olympiad in Informatics):
    • A competitive programming competition hosted by the School of Computing (SoC) of National University of Singapore (NUS).

  • SMU Whitehacks:
    • A well-known competition hosted by the SMU Whitehat society.

  • CSIT-HCI Cyberthon:
    • A capture-the-flag (CTF) competition jointly hosted by CSIT and HCI.
Electronics ▼
  • SAFMC C1

  • SUTD RoboClash

  • NUS HackNRoll

  • NTU EEE Challenge

  • Singapore Robotic Games
Drones ▼
  • SAFMC (Singapore Amazing Flying Machine Competition) C1
    • A competition about designing and building a small remote-controlled fixed-wing air platform to navigate an obstacle course.

  • SAFMC (Singapore Amazing Flying Machine Competition) C2
    • A competition about flying a ducted (shielded propeller) first-person view (FPV) drone to compete in an obstacle course.
VEX ▼
  • WSA VEX Robotics Workshop:
    • An event to learn more about VEX robotics and develop robotics skills while exploring the vast opportunities in STEM education

  • Singapore VRC National Championship 2024:
    • An 1-day skills competition for VEX members to find their drive and passion in the field of robotics.

  • Singapore North VRC Skill Challenge:
    • A locally hosted VEX robotics competition, for students to test their skills and technique.

  • Singapore South VRC Skill Challenge:
    • A locally hosted VEX robotics competition, for students to test their skills and technique.
